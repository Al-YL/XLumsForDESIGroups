Based on the group catalog using DESI DR9 (+Y1) galaxy samples, 
we use the eROSITA Final Equatorial-Depth Survey (eFEDS) to measure 
the X-ray luminosities of 600311 groups using two different algorithms 
in the overlap region of the two observations.

========================================================

1. Group ID;
2. Richness;
3-4. RA & DEC (Luminosity weighted Group Center);
5-6. RA & DEC of BGG;
7-8. RA & DEC (X-ray Center);
9. Halo Mass in log M_halo/(M_{\odot}/h); 
10. Group Redshift;
11. Source Name in the eFEDS Main and Supplementary Catalog;
12. Mean Exposure Time;
13&14. Source Count Rate for each group using Mean and Patrol Background Subtraction, respectively ('2' for Patrol, the same hereinafter);
15&16. Signal-to-noise;
17. The Galactic Hydrogen Column density;
18. ECF that convert the rest-frame 0.1 − 2.4 keV band flux with galactic absorption correction to the 0.2 − 2.3 keV count rate;
19. ECF that convert the rest-frame 0.5 − 2.0 keV band flux with galactic absorption correction to the 0.2 − 2.3 keV count rate;
20. Rest-frame 0.1 − 2.4 keV band Luminosity (Mean Background Subtraction);
21. Rest-frame 0.5 − 2.0 keV band Luminosity (Mean Background Subtraction);
22. Correction factor for Patrol Background Subtraction Algorithm;
23. Rest-frame 0.1 − 2.4 keV band Luminosity (Patrol Background Subtraction);
24. Rest-frame 0.5 − 2.0 keV band Luminosity (Patrol Background Subtraction);
